Piper

Though calm in the arms of friendly faces, Piper can erupt into a ferocious snarl when accosted by strangers.

  • Habitat: On top of the couch pillows
  • Diet: Whatever the human's are eating looks good
  • Favorite Artist: Snoop Dog
  • SuperPower: Combo of greatest lap dog, and most ferocious 18lb guard dog

Eye contact with the young pup is to be avoided(if she doesn't know, and like you). Treats are key to winning her over.
